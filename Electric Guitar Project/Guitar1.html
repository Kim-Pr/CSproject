    (Blues)




    (Stratocaster Guitar)




    A Stratocaster electric guitar is used mainly for the blues genre. What makes them a top choice for blues music are their three single coil pickups That help give this guitar its light, fluent, and clean sound. They are also very easy to play because they normally do not have as many extra features as other electric guitars would have. This is why they also serve as a great choice for people beginning to play electric guitar.
