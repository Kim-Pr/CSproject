This project is on different types of electric guitars, the artists that play them, the genre they are used for and a description of them that explains what makes them essential for the specific genre of music they are used for. The guitars that are featured on this website are the stratocaster, les paul, Esp Ltd and telecaster guitars all used in different musical genres for their different characteristics.
